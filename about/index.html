About

The Dayton Data Visualization meetup was started by Michael Bowman and Chris Dickman of Applied Information Sciences in November of 2014 as a way to gather Dayton area data visualization experts to network and share knowledge. Since then, we’ve grown to 300 members and have hosted a number of great speakers on a wide variety of topics.

You can register for the group and join us at our events from our meetup page. We’d love to see you at our next event.

The Dayton Data Visualization group is a quarterly meetup for data visualization practicioners in the Dayton, Ohio area.

Join us on Meetup.com to RSVP for our upcoming events and receive news and announcements regarding the group!

This site was built with jekyll and is hosted using github pages. We could really use your help generating site content and bug fixing. See a current list of items we need help with on our github issues page. Join the #daytondv channel on the OpenDayton Slack Community and we'll help you get started!
